Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Know Yourself. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our psychology quiz website.

2. Information We Collect

Know Yourself does not collect, store, or share any personal information. Specifically:

  • Your quiz answers are processed locally in your browser only
  • No answers or results are sent to any server
  • No accounts or sign-ups are required
  • No cookies are set by this website

3. Google AdSense & Third-Party Ads

This website may use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense may use cookies to show relevant ads. You can opt out at adssettings.google.com

4. Google Analytics

We may use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use this site. It collects anonymous usage data only and does not collect personally identifiable information.

5. Children's Privacy

Know Yourself is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13.

6. Disclaimer

All quizzes are for self-reflection and entertainment purposes only. They are not clinical assessments and should not be used as a substitute for professional mental health advice.

7. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be directed via the contact information on our website.
